2. Word Choice & Phrasing
"programadores de redes sociales" (small business tool list)
This reads ambiguously — programadores typically means programmers (people) in Spanish. The intended meaning (scheduling tools) is lost. Suggested alternatives: herramientas de programación de redes sociales or planificadores de publicaciones en redes sociales.
"mercadeo por correo electrónico"
Mercadeo is valid but regional — it's most natural in Colombian and Venezuelan Spanish. For a multi-region Spanish audience, marketing por correo electrónico or email marketing would land more universally. Worth a note to the publisher on target regional variant.
"nunca suben lo suficiente como para convertirse en grandes proyectos"
Slightly literal. A more natural construction: "nunca llegan a escalar hasta convertirse en grandes proyectos" or "nunca alcanzan la magnitud de un proyecto de ingeniería formal."
"fuente de verdad"
The translation is consistent and understandable in tech contexts. However, a note: native Spanish tech professionals frequently leave this as "fuente única de verdad" (mirroring the full English phrase single source of truth) or even use the English term directly. If the book introduces this as a defined term, it would be worth a brief inline glossary note on first use.

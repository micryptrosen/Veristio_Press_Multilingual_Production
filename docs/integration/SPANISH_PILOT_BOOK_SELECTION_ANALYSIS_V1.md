## Top Recommendation
Top candidate: `CANDIDATE-001` - Human Creativity + AI Synthesis.

Final recommendation: SELECT_WITH_CONDITIONS

Book 1 is the best first Spanish pilot candidate because it is the most mature inspected repo, has a complete core narrative, has reader and 6x9 review-copy production evidence, is broad and human-facing, and is less technically dense than the quantum, coding, infrastructure, and source-heavy books. It is likely to prove the reusable multilingual workflow without forcing the first pilot to solve the hardest terminology and citation problems at the same time.

## Conditional Requirements Before Packet Preparation
- Source Book repo must identify the pilot chapter or chapter set without moving canonical English authority into this repo.
- Selected chapter(s) must be marked `EN_TRANSLATION_READY` in the handoff packet.
- Source branch, source commit, source chapter path, and English lock version must be recorded.
- Book 1 must declare unresolved claims, source notes, citations, figures, tables, captions, permissions, and rights issues for the selected chapter(s).
- Spanish terminology gaps must be logged before packet admission; the existing EN-ES register is only a seed.
- Spanish reviewer availability must be confirmed or logged as a blocking gap.
- Metadata/ISBN readiness must be tracked as separate Spanish edition work; final release remains blocked until final sync, proof, metadata, ISBN, and release gates pass.

## Why Lower-Ranked Candidates Were Not First
- Book 2 is broad and mature, but source/citation readiness is explicitly blocked until citation placement and final source/citation QA complete.
- Book 11 has excellent education/workforce usefulness, but its chapter material remains draft-candidate, non-canonical, and not source-verified.
- Book 3 has substantial body progress, but quantum-heavy terminology, glossary work, and source-sensitive terms make it a better later pilot.
- Book 12 has strong learner value, but code/quantum scope and source-gated chapter status make it too technical for first process proof.
- Book 4 has meaningful communications value, but active targeted source review and non-final chapter posture keep it behind Book 1.
- Book 7, Book 9, and Book 10 are valuable but carry higher technical, safety, infrastructure, quantum, and citation-verification burden.
- Book 8A, Book 8B, Book 5, and Book 6 are not first-pilot candidates because repo truth shows no chapter-level translation intake readiness.

# Book 1 Spanish Provisional Review Boundary V1

## Purpose

Define what provisional internal review may and may not do for the Book 1 Spanish pilot before named reviewers are assigned.

## Boundary Decision

`PROVISIONAL_INTERNAL_REVIEW_ALLOWED_FOR_DRAFT_ONLY`

## Allowed Under This Boundary

- Prepare the next Spanish translation draft packet as a governed packet artifact.
- Carry the terminology seed as `SEEDED_PENDING_REVIEW`.
- Mark terminology, native-language, technical, and final sync/proof review as pending.
- Keep metadata/ISBN/legal/distribution/release status pending.
- Preserve Book 1 English authority and locked-source identity.

## Not Allowed Under This Boundary

- Inventing named human reviewers.
- Claiming terminology review completion.
- Claiming native-language review completion.
- Claiming technical review completion.
- Claiming final sync/proof completion.
- Claiming proof, metadata, ISBN, release, or publication readiness.
- Editing Book 1 or changing canonical English source text.

## Required Human/Reviewer Signoffs Before Release

- Terminology reviewer signoff.
- Native-language reviewer signoff.
- Technical reviewer signoff.
- Final sync/proof reviewer signoff.
- Metadata/ISBN/release gate approval.

Publication and release remain blocked until the signoffs and release gates above are complete.

## Authority Boundary

Book 1 remains canonical for English manuscript text, readiness, claims, citations, and permissions. Multilingual Production owns the Spanish reviewer workflow, provisional review boundary, packet/tracker status, and language-edition governance.

## Translation Boundary

This boundary document does not create target-language manuscript prose and does not claim Spanish publication readiness.

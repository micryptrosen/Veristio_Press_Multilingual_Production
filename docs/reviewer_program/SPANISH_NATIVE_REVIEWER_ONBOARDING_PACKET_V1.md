# Spanish Native Reviewer Onboarding Packet V1

## Purpose

Prepare a real native-language reviewer to review Book 1 Chapter 1 Spanish Draft V2 when an operator assigns one. This packet is onboarding material only; it does not assign a reviewer and does not perform native-language review.

## Review Scope

- Project: Book 1 Spanish pilot.
- Chapter: Chapter 1 - Work Changed Before Most People Noticed.
- Spanish draft: `docs/packets/book_1_human_creativity_ai_synthesis/BOOK_1_CHAPTER_1_SPANISH_DRAFT_V2.md`.
- Native-review instructions: `docs/packets/book_1_human_creativity_ai_synthesis/BOOK_1_CHAPTER_1_NATIVE_REVIEW_INSTRUCTIONS_V1.md`.

- Locked source commit: `bd0441d4a5cf8e5862a61421b3d515eb079afee4`.

## What The Reviewer Should Do

- Review for natural Spanish.
- Review for neutral Spanish unless a locale is later selected.
- Review tone/register for education, workforce, and professional readers.
- Review title treatment and terms from the terminology seed/register.
- Flag awkward literal translation.
- Flag omitted or added meaning.
- Flag cultural ambiguity.
- Flag wording that needs terminology or technical review.
- Record findings clearly enough for a later revision pass.

## What The Reviewer Must Not Do

- Do not edit Book 1.
- Do not alter the canonical English source.
- Do not claim technical review completion.
- Do not claim final sync/proof approval.
- Do not claim metadata, ISBN, publication, release, or upload readiness.
- Do not redistribute draft material or source-identifying packet material outside authorized channels.

## Confidentiality And IP Boundary

The Spanish draft, packet materials, review checklist, and review notes are Veristio Press production materials. The reviewer must keep them confidential, use them only for the assigned review scope, and avoid copying them into unauthorized public or third-party systems.

## AI-Use Boundary

A reviewer may use AI tools only if the operator authorizes that use for this project and the reviewer can preserve confidentiality/IP obligations. Any AI-assisted review use must be disclosed in the assignment or findings record. AI output cannot substitute for the reviewer signoff.

## Required Review Record When Performed

- reviewer_name
- reviewer_role
- review_date
- review_scope
- review_status
- findings
- required corrections
- unresolved questions
- publication_release_block_acknowledgement

## Boundary

This onboarding packet does not name a reviewer and does not convert the current hold into review execution. Publication remains blocked until documented native review occurs and all downstream gates pass.
